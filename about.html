OUR STORY

At Victorian Crystal Event, we are fueled by a passion for crafting unforgettable experiences that bring people together and create lasting memories.

Our journey began three years ago when our founder, Ms. Victoria Ibeabuchi, set out to build an event planning company that would redefine excellence in the industry.

Backed by over six years of experience in event planning, coordination, and execution, our team has grown into a trusted name for delivering exceptional events.

Guided by the values of creativity, precision, and genuine care, we approach every event with a deep commitment to excellence. What truly drives us is the joy of seeing our clients’ dreams come to life—each celebration uniquely tailored to reflect their story and vision.

To date, we have successfully executed over 100 events, working with a diverse range of clients and bringing each vision to life with unmatched attention to detail.

As we look ahead, our goal is to continue pushing boundaries in the event space, making meaningful impacts and creating experiences that linger in hearts long after the final guest has gone.

We are honored to share our story—and even more excited to creating unforgettable experiences for you.
